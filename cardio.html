Cardio

Boost Your Heart Health and Burn Calories

Cardio exercises increase your heart rate, improve stamina, and help burn calories.

Benefits of Cardio

  • Improves heart health
  • Enhances mood
  • Weight loss
  • Better endurance

Tips for an Effective Cardio Workout

  • Warmup
  • Dynamic stretching
  • Choose proper footwear
  • Set goals and intentions

Hydrate before, during, and after.

  • Start slow and build up
  • Follow the talk test
  • Interval training
  • Listen to your body
  • Progress at your own pace

Types of Cardio Workouts

Cycling
Running
Swimming
Jumprope
