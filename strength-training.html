Strength Training

Build Power and Confidence

Strength training involves exercises designed to improve muscular strength and endurance using resistance, including weights, bodyweight, or bands.

Benefits of Strength Training

  • Improves bone density
  • Boosts metabolism and promotes fat loss
  • Enhances overall physical performance
  • Reduces the risk of injuries by strengthening joints and connective tissues

Strength Training Tips and Techniques

Focus on proper form instead of heavier weight. Get the form down first and then increase the weight to prevent injuries.

You can gradually increase the resistance, repititions, or intensity to continue building strength after you are able to consistently lift a certain weight.

It is recommended to rest in between sets for 30-90 seconds for endurance and hypertrophy and 2-5 minutes for strength and power.
