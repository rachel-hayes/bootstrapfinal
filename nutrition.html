Nutrition

Eat Right, Live Strong

Nutrition supports overall health and fitness so that the body can function optimally, repair tissues, produce energy, and prevent diseases.

Benefits of Proper Nutrition

  • Provides energy for daily activities and workouts
  • Supports muscle repair and recovery
  • Boosts immune function and mental clarity
  • Helps maintain a healthy weight and reduce chronic disease risk

Nutrition for Fitness Goals

  • Focus on calorie deficit through portion control and nutrient-dense foods
  • Prioritize lean proteins, high-fiber vegetables, and healthy fats

  • Emphasize protein intake (chicken, fish, tofu) and complex carbs (brown rice, oats)
  • Incorporate calorie-dense, healthy options like nut butter, avocados, and smoothies

  • Include more carbohydrates for sustained energy (e.g., whole grains, fruits, sweet potatoes)
  • Hydration and electrolytes are key

  • Aim for a well-rounded diet with variety and moderation
  • Avoid highly processed foods and excessive added sugars
