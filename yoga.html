Yoga

Balance Your Body and Mind

Yoga is a mind-body practice combining physical postures, breathing techniques, and meditation.

Benefits of Yoga

Physical Benefits

  • Increases flexibility and range of motion
  • Builds strength in muscles and joints
  • Enhances cardiovascular health through dynamic flows
  • Relieves chronic pain, especially in the back and neck

Mental and Emotional Benefits

  • Reduces anxiety and stress through mindfulness
  • Improves focus and concentration
  • Boosts mood and self-awareness

Tips for a Safe and Effective Yoga Practice

Proper alignment prevents injury and maximizes benefits.

Use props or adjust poses to suit your flexibility and strength.

Rest if you feel discomfort or pain; yoga should never be forced.

Types of Yoga

Hatha Yoag

Gentle and beginner-friendly, focusing on basic poses and breathing.

Vinyasa Yoga

A dynamic, flow-based style that links breath with movement.

Ashtanga Yoga

A more structured, physically intense style with a set sequence of poses.

Yin Yoga

Focuses on deep stretching and holding poses for longer periods.

Restorative Yoga

Emphasizes relaxation and uses props to support the body.

Power Yoga

A high-intensity, fitness-focused style.

Hot/Bikran Yoga

Practiced in a heated room for enhanced flexibility and detoxification.
